DIGITAL BRIDGE
AWARDS 2023
Applications are open

Digital Bridge Awards is a prestigious Central Asian award held to recognize technology companies and startup teams for their digital solutions, tools and methods used, creativity in problem solving, and achievements that have influenced their growth and profitability.

﻿Companies from Central Asian countries - Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Azerbaijan and Mongolia - can be nominated for the Digital Bridge Awards.

Vote for the best IT company!
The nomination is awarded to a team that shows significant results such as increased users / market share / revenue over the last 12 months, and applies AI technologies in their projects
The nomination is awarded to the organization that has achieved the greatest success in exporting IT goods / IT services
The nomination is awarded to a programming school that has demonstrated significant results (in terms of number of students graduated, number of students employed, etc.) and applies innovative, bold and creative initiatives
The nomination is awarded to a venture capitalist who has made a significant impact by demonstrating excellence in identifying and supporting innovative startup projects
The nomination is awarded to a media resource that has effectively used media tools, opened new channels of interaction with the audience, and ensured their engagement
The nomination is awarded to an organization that uses technology to improve the lives of others and the world around us, and solves environmental, social, economic problems
The nomination is awarded to an organization that successfully identifies and meets customer needs and continually improves engagement with its target audience
The nomination is awarded to an organization that creates a stimulating working environment for professional and personal growth of its employees and adheres to high standards in the field of personnel development
