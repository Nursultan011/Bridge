QUESTION:
What is Pitching Zone Digital Bridge 2023?
ANSWER:
As part of the Digital Bridge IT forum, which will be held on October 12-13 in Astana, a dialogue platform will be organized for representatives of large businesses, the state and startups

Representatives of companies will have the opportunity to listen to presentations by IT startups in a short period and get acquainted with the latest innovations and technologies that will help improve their business and make it more competitive in the market.

Format: 1 on 1 in the form of speed networking for 5 minutes.
